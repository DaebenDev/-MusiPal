About MusiPal

MusiPal is developed by a passionate team dedicated to making music creation easy and accessible for everyone, especially aspiring musicians and hobbyists. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly platform where creativity knows no bounds.

Meet Our Passionate Team

Role: Main Developer / Tech Lead

Responsibilities: Frontend & backend development, integrating music generation tools/libraries, and ensuring everything runs smoothly.

Role: UI/UX Designer

Responsibilities: Designing how the site looks and feels, creating wireframes, mockups, and responsive layouts, and ensuring the interface is intuitive and easy to use, especially for music interaction.

Role: Project Manager / QA Tester

Responsibilities: Tracking progress and tasks, scheduling team meetings, testing features, reporting bugs, and ensuring product quality.

Role: Documentation Lead

Responsibilities: Writing technical and user documentation, including how the system works (for developers) and user guides/tutorials (for users), and tracking version changes and setup steps.
