Frequently Asked Questions

MusiPal is a web-based application designed to help aspiring musicians generate music using simple descriptions. It serves as a starter tool for those beginning their musical journey.

Simply type in a short description of the music you want to create, adjust the beat length using the slider, and click "Generate." MusiPal will create a music sample based on your input.

MusiPal is made for anyone, especially beginners and hobbyists who want to explore music creation easily and quickly without needing advanced tools or knowledge.

You can create various styles of music by describing your desired mood, instruments, or genre. Since all our data is non-copyright, you can freely use your generated samples for inspiration or as foundational elements for your own compositions.
